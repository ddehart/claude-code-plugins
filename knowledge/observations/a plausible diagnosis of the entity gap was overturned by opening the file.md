The expected explanation was that entity guidance is deleted at generation time; it fit the symptom that
entities were under-served. Checking showed the guidance survived into the generated skills intact, and the
real defect was narrower and structural — entity creation was reactive, with no signal class of its own.
The same session produced three more of the shape: a coherence conflict conceded as overstated once its
source was traced, two "agent discrepancies" that were the verifier's own measurement errors, and a ledger
read where substring presence matched the expected answer so cleanly the actual test went unchecked.
