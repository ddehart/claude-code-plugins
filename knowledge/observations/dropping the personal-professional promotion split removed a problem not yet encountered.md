An earlier design gated cross-boundary promotion differently depending on a personal/professional
confidentiality posture. It was collapsed to one uniform mechanism, with confidentiality left to the
human-approval gate that already reads every proposed promotion. Derek named the reasoning directly: the
posture split "again prematurely solves a problem we don't have yet." A screening question earns its place
the day a leak actually slips through — not before.
