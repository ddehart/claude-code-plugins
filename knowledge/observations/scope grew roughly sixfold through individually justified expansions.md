The session opened as "fix four documented bugs" and closed with six merged PRs, a new skill, a
22-decision spec, a new source tier, a new attractor type, a global rule change, two documentation fixes, a
logged pre-existing defect and a cleanup pass. No single expansion was unjustified; each followed from
something discovered. The user twice reported losing track — "I'm not sure what I need to do here" and
"I've lost the thread" — and in both cases the immediate trigger was a comprehensive status report
delivered where a single question was needed. The aggregate needed a checkpoint that no individual step
would ever trigger.
