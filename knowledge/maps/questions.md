# Questions

Open attractors — things this graph does not yet know, held so that evidence can accumulate against them
rather than being lost for want of a home. A question asserts nothing beyond "worth watching"; the bar
that protects the graph's authority is graduation, not entry. Entries are annotated:
`- [[title]] — what would settle it, in one clause`.

Distinct from [[patterns]]: a pattern is a shape that recurs and has evidence; a question is an
unresolved matter that may have none yet. A question that accumulates enough evidence graduates — into a
`decision` if it settles, or a `pattern` if what it reveals is a recurring shape.
