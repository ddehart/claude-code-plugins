# the commons

The single cross-domain graph — one instantiation of the same generated mechanism, living at
`~/Developer/commons`. Receive-only: fed exclusively by [[promote skill]] from other domains' graphs, each
claim carrying a `domain:` label. It accumulates promoted claims and open questions, and graduates
steering-grade principles into `~/.claude/rules/` with human approval; a principle becomes "held" only once
its evidence spans more than one domain. It never processes raw material and never reaches into project
trees. See [[the commons is the evidence tier beneath behavioral rules]].
